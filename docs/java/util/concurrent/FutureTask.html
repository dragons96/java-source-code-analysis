During completion, state may take on transient values of COMPLETING (while outcome is being set) or INTERRUPTING (only while interrupting the runner to satisfy a cancel(true)). Transitions from these intermediate to final states use cheaper ordered/lazy writes because values are unique and cannot be further modified.
